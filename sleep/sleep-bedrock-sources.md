# Sources for Sleep Bedrock Facts

## SB1 – Sleep deprivation is fatal
- Montagna P, et al. “Fatal familial insomnia and the thalamus.”
  *Lancet Neurology* 2003. [PubMed: 32502393]
  – Shows that destruction of sleep‑generating brain regions leads to inevitable death.
- Llorens F, et al. “Fatal Familial Insomnia: Clinical and Neurobiological Aspects.”
  *Prion* 2022. [link]
  – Confirms uniform fatality once total insomnia is established.
- Rechtschaffen A, et al. “Physiological correlates of prolonged sleep deprivation in rats.”
  *Science* 1983. [PubMed: 6857253]
  – Foundational animal model: total sleep deprivation is lethal in all mammals tested.

## SB2 – Sleep is entrained by the solar light‑dark cycle
- Yetish G, et al. “Natural sleep and its seasonal variations in three pre‑industrial societies.”
  *Current Biology* 2015. [PubMed: 26480842]
  – Direct field measurement: sleep in groups living without artificial light is consolidated at night.
- Berson DM, et al. “Phototransduction by retinal ganglion cells that set the circadian clock.”
  *Science* 2002. [PubMed: 11834835]
  – Discovery of the light‑sensitive cells that wire the human clock to the sun.
