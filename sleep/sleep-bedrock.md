# Undeniable Facts About Human Sleep

- **SB1** – Prolonged total sleep deprivation is fatal in humans.
- **SB2** – In the absence of artificial light, all human populations sleep predominantly at night and wake during daylight.
- **SB3** – Every healthy human dreams. Dreaming occurs in a specific, cyclically recurring brain state (REM sleep).
- **SB4** – Sleep is structured in repeating cycles (~90 min). Deep sleep dominates early cycles; dream-rich sleep dominates later cycles.
